## Message from Founder and Director

<div class="message-wrap">

  <blockquote class="content">
    <p>"Welcome to the Radiology Informatics and Image Processing Laboratory (RIIPL), a research lab within the Yale School of Medicine Department of Radiology and Biomedical Imaging. We advance medical imaging through computational modeling, artificial intelligence and deep learning, graph theoretical network methods, and high-throughput analytic pipelines, with a core focus on translating these methods into clinical practice.</p>
    <p>Our research spans early neurodevelopment through later adulthood, with active programs in Alzheimer's disease, traumatic brain injury, neurooncology, preterm birth, adolescent nicotine exposure, Parkinson's disease, diabetes, dyslexia, and the impact of metabolic and vascular factors on neurodegeneration. We integrate multi-site datasets, including large-scale initiatives such as the Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative (ADNI), with computational modeling and AI to deepen biological understanding and guide clinical decision-making.</p>
    <p>RIIPL also maintains expertise in medical 3D printing for surgical planning and patient education, and in immersive technologies including virtual reality, extended reality, and spatial computing.</p>
    <p>Our team brings together biomedical engineers, computer scientists, neuroradiologists, and medical physicists alongside graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and undergraduate interns, all united by a commitment to transforming healthcare through imaging science."</p>
  </blockquote>
